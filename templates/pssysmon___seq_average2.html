SEQ_AVERAGE2 (PSSYSMON) Register

SEQ_AVERAGE2 (PSSYSMON) Register Description

Register NameSEQ_AVERAGE2
Relative Address0x000000011C
Absolute Address 0x00FFA5091C (AMS_PS_SYSMON)
Width16
TyperwNormal read/write
Reset Value0x00000000
DescriptionSequencer Average Enable, Group 2.

Enable averaging over multiple readings on a per channel basis. Applies to auto-sequencing. 0: disabled. 1: enabled. The enable and averaging size are selected by Config_Reg0 [13:12].
