LOCAL_GEN_STORAGE0 (PMU_LOCAL) Register

LOCAL_GEN_STORAGE0 (PMU_LOCAL) Register Description

Register NameLOCAL_GEN_STORAGE0
Relative Address0x0000000300
Absolute Address 0x00FFD60300 (PMU_LOCAL)
Width32
TyperwNormal read/write
Reset Value0x00000000
DescriptionPower Supply Status and General Purpose Read-Write.

During boot, this register holds a copy of the PMU_GLOBAL.PWR_SUPPLY_STATUS register. After boot, this register can be used by PMU Firmware as a general storage registers. Its contents are not modified or used by PMU ROM after handoff to the Firmware.
