PMU_BootROM_CTRL (EFUSE) Register Bit-Field Summary

Field NameBitsTypeReset ValueDescription
PBR_BOOT_ERROR 2:0roRead-only0x0Control the behavior of boot upon detected errors:
111: Errors halt the system.
Others: The system will continue to boot with errors.
The presence of errors can be detected on PS_ERROR_OUTPUT and the status of errors can be read from the PMU_GLOBAL.ERROR_STATUS_2 and PMU_GLOBAL.ERROR_STATUS_1 registers.
