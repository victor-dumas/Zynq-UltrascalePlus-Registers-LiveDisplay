PERS_LOC_GEN_STORAGE3 (PMU_LOCAL) Register Description

Register NamePERS_LOC_GEN_STORAGE3
Relative Address0x000000031C
Absolute Address 0x00FFD6031C (PMU_LOCAL)
Width32
TyperwNormal read/write
Reset Value0x00000000
DescriptionPersistent Local General Storage. Reset by POR only.

Persistent Local General Storage Registers that can be used to store information needed by the PMU Firmware. The register is reset only by the POR Reset; it maintains its state through a System Reset.
