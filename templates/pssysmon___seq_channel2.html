SEQ_CHANNEL2 (PSSYSMON) Register

SEQ_CHANNEL2 (PSSYSMON) Register Description

Register NameSEQ_CHANNEL2
Relative Address0x0000000118
Absolute Address 0x00FFA50918 (AMS_PS_SYSMON)
Width16
TyperwNormal read/write
Reset Value0x00000000
DescriptionSequencer Channel Inclusion, Group 2.

Include these channels in the auto-sequencing measurement mechanism performed by the PS SysMon unit. 0: exclude channel. 1: include channel.
