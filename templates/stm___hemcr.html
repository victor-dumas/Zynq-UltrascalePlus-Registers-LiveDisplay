HEMCR (STM) Register Description

Register NameHEMCR

Absolute Address 0x00FE9C0D64 (CORESIGHT_SOC_STM)
Width32
TypemixedMixed types. See bit-field details.

DescriptionControl the primary functions of Hardware Event tracing.
