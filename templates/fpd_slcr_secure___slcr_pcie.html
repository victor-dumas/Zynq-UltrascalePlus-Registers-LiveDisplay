slcr_pcie (FPD_SLCR_SECURE) Register Description

Register Nameslcr_pcie
Relative Address0x0000000030
Absolute Address 0x00FD690030 (FPD_SLCR_SECURE)
Width32
TypemixedMixed types. See bit-field details.
Reset Value0x01FFFFFF
DescriptionPCIe TrustZone settings.

Modify during bootup (while PCIe Gen2 controller is disabled). 0: Secure. 1: Non-Secure.
