SUPPLY6 (PSSYSMON) Register

SUPPLY6 (PSSYSMON) Register Description

Register NameSUPPLY6
Relative Address0x000000003C
Absolute Address 0x00FFA5083C (AMS_PS_SYSMON)
Width16
TyperoRead-only
Reset Value0x00000000
DescriptionPS IO Bank 500 voltage measurement (supply6).

IO Bank 500 for PSIO0 voltage node for MIO[0:25] pins. The voltage measurement is done with a unipolar sampling circuit to produce an unsigned result with a 0 to 6V range.
