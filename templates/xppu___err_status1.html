ERR_STATUS1 (XPPU) Register

ERR_STATUS1 (XPPU) Register Description

Register NameERR_STATUS1
Relative Address0x0000000004
Absolute Address 0x00FF980004 (LPD_XPPU)
Width32
TyperoRead-only
Reset Value0x00000000
DescriptionError Status, Reg 1. Poisoned address.

The first violation is recorded. Once an ISR[7:1] status bit is set, subsequent violations are not recorded, but their transactions are poisoned. The status bits are cleared by a system reset and can be cleared by software.
