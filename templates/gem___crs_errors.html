crs_errors (GEM) Register Bit-Field Summary

Field NameBitsTypeReset ValueDescription
Reserved31:10roRead-only0x0Reserved, read as 0, ignored on write.
count 9:0rwNormal read/write0x0Carrier sense errors - a 10 bit register counting the number of frames transmitted where carrier sense was not seen during transmission or where carrier sense was deasserted after being asserted in a transmit frame without collision (no under run). Only incremented in half duplex mode. The only effect of a carrier sense error is to increment this register. The behaviour of the other statistics registers is unaffected by the detection of a carrier sense error.
