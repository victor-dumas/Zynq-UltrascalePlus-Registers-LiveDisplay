SUPPLY7 (PLSYSMON) Register

SUPPLY7 (PLSYSMON) Register Description

Register NameSUPPLY7
Relative Address0x0000000200
Absolute Address 0x00FFA50E00 (AMS_PL_SYSMON)
Width16
TyperoRead-only
Reset Value0x00000000
DescriptionVUser0 Voltage measurement (supply7).

The voltage measurement is done with a unipolar sampling circuit to produce an unsigned result with a 0 to 6V range.
